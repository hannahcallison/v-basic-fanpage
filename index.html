Paramore Fan Site


Paramore is an alternative band who has been active since 2004! They are most well known to emo/pop punk kids who have been listening to them since the mid 2000's. Paramore is still an active band and is planning on releasing another album soon, they are currently in the studio.

Active members:

  • Haley Williams, lead vocalist
  • Taylor York, guitarist
  • Zac Farro, drummer
